Cookie Policy

How we use cookies (spoiler: we don't).

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device by websites. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

How FairSplit Uses Cookies

FairSplit does not use cookies. We do not store any information about your usage, preferences, or personal data. Your browsing experience is completely anonymous and private.

No Tracking

We do not use any tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or advertising cookies. We do not track your behavior or collect data about your usage patterns.

No Third-Party Cookies

We do not allow third parties to place cookies on your device through our website. No advertising networks, analytics services, or social media platforms have access to your data through FairSplit.

No LocalStorage or Similar Technologies

FairSplit does not use localStorage, sessionStorage, or any other client-side storage technologies to store personal data. All calculations are performed in-memory and are not persisted.

Browser Settings

If you have concerns about cookies, you can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies. Since FairSplit does not use cookies, this will not affect your ability to use our service.

Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Cookie Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Cookie Policy, please contact us through our contact page.
