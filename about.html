Fair bill splitting, made simple.

What is FairSplit?

FairSplit Bill Tool is a free, simple, and fast bill-splitting calculator designed to help groups of people divide shared expenses fairly. How FairSplit Works

FairSplit offers three main ways to split bills:

1. Simple Split

Perfect for when everyone orders roughly the same amount. Enter the total bill, tax, tip, and number of people. FairSplit instantly calculates how much each person owes.

2. Itemized Split

For more complex situations where people ordered different amounts. Add each person's name, then list each item and assign it to whoever ordered it. FairSplit calculates each person's fair share of the subtotal, tax, and tip.

3. Who Pays? Game

Can't decide who should pay for everyone? Let luck decide! The "Who Pays?" game randomly selects one lucky person to cover the entire bill.
