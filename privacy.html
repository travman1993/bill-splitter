Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us.

Introduction

FairSplit Bill Tool ("we", "our", or "us") operates the FairSplit website. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our service.

Information Collection and Use

FairSplit collects no personal data. Your bill calculations and information are processed entirely in your browser and never transmitted to our servers. We do not store, collect, or track any information about you or your usage.

No Cookies

FairSplit does not use cookies or any tracking technologies. Your browsing experience is completely private and anonymous.

No Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking tools. All calculations happen locally in your browser.

Security of Data

Since all data is processed locally in your browser, there is no transmission or storage of sensitive information. Your data never leaves your device.

GDPR Compliance

FairSplit is fully compliant with GDPR regulations. We do not collect, process, or store any personal data, so there are no data privacy concerns.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our contact page.
